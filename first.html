PrakhaR Tripathi

Prakhar Tripathi is a writer, director, and photographer known for his compelling storytelling across film and visual media. He wrote and directed the acclaimed short film Zara Si Dhoop, featuring Sheeba Chaddha, Plabita Borthakur, and Vishwas Kini. He gained widespread recognition for conceptualizing and directing Gujarat Tourism's iconic Kutch Mein, Haan Sach Mein campaign, which beautifully captured the essence of the region. His diverse portfolio includes ad films for major brands like Ambuja Cement, Mankind Pharma, HDFC Life, Blue Star, and Airtel. Many of his commercials have been widely praised, earning both critical acclaim and a strong audience response. Prakhar's journey began behind the lens as a photographer, with his work featured in Asian Geographic, National Geographic, and Ink - We Are Travel Media. His photography has been showcased in several global exhibitions, reflecting his keen artistic vision and deep understanding of visual storytelling. He is the founder of Sharpcom Media, a film production and advertising agency. - IMDb mini biography by: Sharpcom
